Reverse Sear New York Strip

Description

While most prefer steak cooked over a fire or a grill, when weather and circumstances don't allow, a reverse sear steak is the next best thing. Easy and perfect every time! Juicy and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1 1/2 inch thick) New York Strip steaks
  • 4 Tablespoon butter
  • Your preferred seasoning. We use Steak and Chop

Steps

  1. Put the seasoned steak in a low oven and let it cook slowly until it reaches your desired internal temperature. Use a thermometer probe to check the internal temperature for doneness. This could take up to an hour.
  2. Sear the steak in a cast iron, carbon steel, or heavy stainless steel pan when smoking hot. It probably needs no more than a minute on each side.
  3. Baste the steak with butter until the steak has a crisp, dark exterior
